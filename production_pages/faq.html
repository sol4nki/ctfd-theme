Frequently Asked Questions

What is the flag format?

All flags follow the format: d4rk{flag_content}c0de. Please ensure you submit the exact string, including the braces.

How do I report a broken challenge?

If a challenge seems broken or unreachable, please contact an admin via our official support channel with the challenge name and a brief description of the issue.

Are we allowed to collaborate?

Collaboration within your registered team is encouraged. However, sharing flags between different teams is strictly prohibited and will result in disqualification.

Can I use automated tools?

Standard security tools are fine, but excessive automated scanning or brute-forcing that impacts the stability of our infrastructure is forbidden.
