Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for a team?

You can create or join a team through the 'Teams' tab in the navigation bar after logging in.

What if I find a bug in a challenge?

Please report it immediately to the admins. Include the challenge name and a screenshot of the issue if possible.

Are hints available?

Some challenges have hints available. Check the challenge description—some may cost points to unlock.
